iPhone vs Samsung: Which Should You Get?

iPhone 15 Pro vs Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra - Complete Comparison

Apple: The $2.8 Trillion Tech Giant

Founded: 1976 in a garage by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne

First iPhone: 2007 (changed phones forever)

Today: Worth $2.8 trillion (richest company in the world)

Fun Fact: Sells over 200 million iPhones every year

Samsung: The $370 Billion Korean Powerhouse

Founded: 1938 as a grocery store (yes, really!)

First Galaxy Phone: 2009 (to compete with iPhone)

Today: Worth $370 billion

Fun Fact: Makes everything from phones to washing machines to skyscrapers

Apple iPhone 15 Pro

What's Good:

  • • Super smooth and easy to use
  • • Really good cameras (especially for Instagram)
  • • All your Apple stuff works together perfectly
  • • Holds its value if you want to sell it later
  • • Gets updates for like 5+ years

What's Not So Good:

  • • Expensive (starts at $999)
  • • Doesn't come with a charger anymore
  • • Not much you can change about how it looks
  • • Battery could be better

Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

What's Good:

  • • Bigger, brighter screen (great for videos)
  • • Comes with a cool pen (S Pen) for drawing/notes
  • • More camera options (200MP is crazy)
  • • You can customize how it looks more than iPhone
  • • Battery lasts longer

What's Not So Good:

  • • Even more expensive (starts at $1,299)
  • • Big and heavy (might not fit in small pockets)
  • • Doesn't work as well with Apple stuff
  • • Won't get updates as long as iPhone
