iPhone vs Samsung: Which Should You Get?

iPhone 15 Pro vs Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra - Complete Comparison

Apple: The Tech Giant Behind iPhone

Founded: April 1, 1976 in Cupertino, California by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne

First iPhone: Released June 29, 2007 - revolutionized smartphones with touchscreen and apps

Market Value: $2.8 trillion (most valuable company in the world)

iPhone Sales: Over 230 million iPhones sold annually

Fun Fact: The original iPhone development cost $150 million and was kept secret even from most Apple employees

Samsung: From Fish Export to Phone Giant

Founded: 1938 in South Korea as a trading company exporting fish and groceries

First Galaxy Phone: June 2009 (Galaxy i7500) to compete with iPhone

Market Value: $370 billion

Galaxy Sales: Over 250 million smartphones sold annually

Fun Fact: Samsung makes components used in iPhones, including screens and chips, while competing with Apple in phones

Apple iPhone 15 Pro

What's Good:

  • • Super smooth and easy to use
  • • Really good cameras (especially for Instagram)
  • • All your Apple stuff works together perfectly
  • • Holds its value if you want to sell it later
  • • Gets updates for like 5+ years

What's Not So Good:

  • • Expensive (starts at $999)
  • • Doesn't come with a charger anymore
  • • Not much you can change about how it looks
  • • Battery could be better

Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra

What's Good:

  • • Bigger, brighter screen (great for videos)
  • • Comes with a cool pen (S Pen) for drawing/notes
  • • More camera options (200MP is crazy)
  • • You can customize how it looks more than iPhone
  • • Battery lasts longer

What's Not So Good:

  • • Even more expensive (starts at $1,299)
  • • Big and heavy (might not fit in small pockets)
  • • Doesn't work as well with Apple stuff
  • • Won't get updates as long as iPhone

Which Phone Should You Get?

Get the iPhone 15 Pro if:

  • • You already have other Apple stuff (Mac, iPad, AirPods)
  • • You want something simple that just works
  • • You care about resale value
  • • Most of your friends have iPhones (iMessage!)
  • • You prefer iOS over Android

Get the Samsung S24 Ultra if:

  • • You want the biggest, most powerful Android phone
  • • You like to customize your phone's look
  • • You'll actually use the S Pen for notes/drawing
  • • You prefer Android's flexibility
  • • You want the best camera specs on paper

Our Recommendation:

The iPhone 15 Pro is better for most people because:

  1. 1. It's $300 cheaper than the S24 Ultra
  2. 2. Works perfectly with other Apple products
  3. 3. Gets updates for 5+ years (Samsung gets about 4)
  4. 4. Easier to get help at Apple Stores
  5. 5. Holds its value better if you sell it later

But consider the S24 Ultra if:

  • • You really want that huge screen and S Pen
  • • You prefer Android's customization options
  • • You take lots of photos and want 200MP
  • • You don't mind the bigger size and weight
